## Product Archive Pages

Product archives should use category filters and inquiry-oriented product cards. Cards should not show prices or Add to Cart actions. Use Inquiry or Request Quote buttons linking to `/request-quote/`.

## Product Detail Pages

Product pages should focus on B2B inquiry, not retail checkout. Recommended fields:

- Product Name
- Product Category
- Material
- Size / Capacity
- Color Options
- Logo Options
- Packaging Options
- MOQ
- Sample Lead Time
- Bulk Production Time
- Amazon FBA Support
- Custom Branding
- Request Quote

## Customization Pages

Customization pages should cover:

- Custom Logos
- Private Label Packaging
- Custom Colors & Materials
- OEM/ODM Manufacturing
- Low MOQ Customization

Each page should explain options, process, requirements, and Request Quote CTA.

## Amazon FBA Page

The Amazon FBA page should explain labeling, packaging, carton planning, shipment preparation, and supplier coordination for Amazon sellers.
