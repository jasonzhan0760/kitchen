# Content Plan

## Brand Voice

DESIOLE Kitchen should sound professional, direct, export-ready, and practical. The copy should speak to wholesalers, importers, distributors, Amazon sellers, and brand owners who need reliable kitchen tools supply from China.

Avoid retail hype, marketplace language, exaggerated discounts, and unsupported claims.

## Product Content Rules

Product copy should be inquiry-oriented and B2B-focused. Include practical specification fields and avoid consumer checkout language.

Recommended fields:

- Product Name
- Product Category
- Material
- Size / Capacity
- Color Options
- Logo Options
- Packaging Options
- MOQ
- Sample Lead Time
- Bulk Production Time
- Amazon FBA Support
- Custom Branding
- Request Quote
