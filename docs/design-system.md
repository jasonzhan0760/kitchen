# Design System

## Visual Direction

The final direction is a modern B2B red + charcoal wholesale kitchenware supplier site. The design should feel factory-direct, professional, export-oriented, and confidence-building rather than retail, marketplace, or cheap promotional.

The homepage should follow the third selected design:

- Large hero with a full kitchen tools product set
- Strong black/red contrast
- Factory-direct data strip under the hero
- Product category cards
- Service cards for customization, Amazon FBA, and quality control
- Featured products section
- FAQ section
- Red final CTA band
- Dark professional footer

## Colors

- Primary Red: `#B5161B`
- Deep Red: `#8F1015`
- Charcoal: `#222222`
- Dark Footer: `#151719`
- Warm White: `#FFF8F3`
- Light Gray: `#F5F5F5`
- Border Gray: `#E5E7EB`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

Use a clean system sans-serif stack for performance and readability.

- Headings: strong, compact, professional, with clear hierarchy
- Body: readable B2B copy with short paragraphs
- Navigation: medium-weight, concise labels
- Buttons: bold enough to scan quickly, sentence case or title case

Avoid decorative typefaces and oversized marketing-style copy outside the homepage hero.

## Layout

- Use a max content width around `1180px`.
- Keep sections full-width with constrained inner content.
- Use responsive grids for categories, service cards, products, and footer columns.
- Keep card radius restrained at `8px` or less.
- Use clear spacing, strong alignment, and high scanability.
- Maintain mobile-first responsive behavior with no text overlap.

## Components

Core components:

- Top bar with export/B2B trust messaging and contact email
- Header navigation with right-side Request Quote CTA
- Hero CTA buttons
- Factory-direct data strip
- Product category cards
- Service cards
- Featured product cards
- FAQ rows
- Red CTA band
- Dark footer link columns

Buttons:

- Primary buttons use Primary Red or Deep Red with white text.
- Secondary buttons use white or transparent surfaces with clear borders.
- Product and inquiry buttons should lead to `/request-quote/`.

## Images And Logos

Use the DESIOLE Kitchen brand name in text until final logo assets are provided.

Homepage hero image:

- Use a full kitchen tools product set.
- Keep a clean export catalog feel.
- Avoid retail lifestyle scenes, people, clutter, text inside images, watermarks, phone numbers, and unrelated brand names.
